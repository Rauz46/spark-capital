Our Unique 3 Phase Startup Investing Model

Our investing model takes a startup from ideation to MVP to revenue and beyond. We do this through a unique 3 phase startup investing model.

Phase 1 - Pre-seed Investing via Fund 1

Fund 1 invests and initially owns 100% of new startups incubated via our Venture Studio; and builds the startups from idea to $1M ARR. Post which the startup exits our Venture Studio and moves to Phase 2 of investing.

Phase 2 - Seed Investing via Fund 2

Fund 2 invests in Phase 2 and takes 20-30% equity in the startup, with another 10-20% equity made available for a proven sales leader to take on the CEO role and to accelerate the startup to multimillion revenue.

Phase 3 - Series A Investing via External Investors

Phase 3 is when External Investors enter the startup's cap table. During this phase the startup will be on track to multimillion revenue and starts to operate fully independently as a fast growing tech company.

Our Story

Spark Capital was born out of our frustration as Angel Investors, having seen way too many startups perish at the infamous Death Valley curve. Our core team's extensive experience in bootstrapping and growing tech startups to multimillion revenue, led us to a new way to invest and incubate B2B tech startups. It taught us the importance of execution over the ideas itself, the criticality of getting to paying customers quickly and the necessity to scale the startup beyond $1million ARR. Our investment model via Venture Studio is based on these learning and our early success with some of our startups getting to multimillion in annuity revenues.

Our Funds

Our capital strategy is built to confront the hardest phase of startup risk head-on. Fund 1 absorbs early uncertainty through ownership, control, and deep validation inside our Venture Studio. Fund 2 enters once the path forward is clear, revenue is established, and growth becomes repeatable. This structure allows risk to fall before capital scales.

Open to LPs

Fund 2

Having seen success with Fund 1, we are now on our 2nd Fund and continuing to onboard Limited Partners or LPs to Fund 2. With Fund 2, our aim is to enable LPs to participate in startup investing after early validation and revenue milestones are achieved. This means, Fund 2 comes in as growth capital for the startups and also the transition to a new leader for the next phase of the startups growth. Fund 2 takes 20 to 30% equity, with 10 to 20% allocated to incoming leadership, resulting in a planned and transparent dilution of Fund 1.
